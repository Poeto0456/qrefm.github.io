The Quantitative Resources for Economic and Financial Modeling (QREFM Lab) is a specialized unit in the research and application of mathematical tools to build economic and financial models. We are dedicated to fostering innovation and guiding the next generation of researchers.

Welcome to QREFM Lab

Shaping the future of quantitative research.

Our lab is committed to excellence. We strive to build an internationally oriented research environment, conduct cutting-edge academic research, and provide comprehensive guidance to students and trainees in implementing theoretical and applied research projects.

Furthermore, we actively support our students in prestigious international competitions and in their applications for scholarships to study abroad, ensuring they have the resources and backing to achieve their full potential.

Research Directions

  • Research on quantitative finance (pricing, hedging, trading).
  • Research on mathematical models of market microstructure.
  • Research on modern decision-making models and game theory.
  • Research on macroeconomic models incorporating climate change and risk.
  • Application of machine learning tools to complex economic models.
  • Combining mathematical tools and economic theories for market efficiency, collective decision-making, pricing, advertising, and optimal contract design.
